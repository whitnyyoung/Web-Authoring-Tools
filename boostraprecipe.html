Ingredients

  • Kashi, Go Lean, Toasted Berry Krisp Cereal
  • Vanilla Silk Soy Milk
  • Honey
  • Raisins
  • Granola (optional)
  • 1 Red Cup (doesn't have to be Solo)

What to Do:

  1. Take out a cup. If you really want to be like me, it should be a red cup.
  2. Put the cereal in your cup. You can pour as much as you would like. Same for the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Next, add the raisins, or whatever dried fruit/ add-in you would like.
  4. Next, add the honey.
  5. Pour the milk over the honey. Try to pour the milk over all the honey, so that the top looks milky.
  6. Lastly, mix everything together!

Taylored Cereal

by Taylor Roper

I came up with this cereal-elevating receipe when I wanted cereal but did not want it to be basic. I also wanted my cereal to be sweeter, hence the honey. So, I looked in my fridge to see what I could use, and these extra ingredients are what I had. This is catered to my taste buds, so feel free to customize this recipe to your liking. I make this when I'm hungry.
Also, the red cup included in the ingredients is becuase I love eating out of cups, and I really feel that eating out of cups elevates the experience. Try it! You never know. You may soon love to eat out of cups as much as I do.
