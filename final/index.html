About Me

Hi! My name is Taylor, and welcome to my site! If you look at the menu in the upper right corner, you will see that I have two different types of blogs. I really want to challenge myself to create thoughtful content and themes when I am writing. If you feel that I am doing that, let me know, and if not, let me know as well!
