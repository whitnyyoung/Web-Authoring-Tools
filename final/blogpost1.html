Korean Blog

Welcome to my blog! My name is Taylor Roper, and if you couldn’t tell, I love Korean dramas. I also love discussing them, so this is what this blog will be about. I will write about the Korean drama that I am currently watching and my thoughts about them. I will talk about different themes I see in them and my speculations about what is to come. Feel free to comment on my posts and express your thoughts and opinions as well. I love a good discussion, and my intentions were to create a space where discussions should take place. Until then, 안녕히 가세요!
